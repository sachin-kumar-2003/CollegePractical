Course Structure

The Master of Computer Applications (MCA) program at Graphic Era University is designed to provide students with a comprehensive understanding of computer applications and software development.

Semester Subjects
1st Semester Mathematics, Computer Programming, Computer Organization
2nd Semester Data Structures, Software Engineering, Database Management Systems
3rd Semester Web Technologies, Operating Systems, Network Programming
4th Semester Mobile Application Development, Cloud Computing, Project Management
5th Semester Artificial Intelligence, Data Mining, Cyber Security
6th Semester Major Project, Internship, Electives

About the MCA Program

The MCA program is aimed at preparing professionals to meet the demands of the IT industry. Our curriculum is regularly updated to keep pace with the latest technological advancements.

The program focuses on both theoretical knowledge and practical skills, ensuring that students are well-equipped to tackle real-world challenges. Students engage in various projects, internships, and workshops that provide hands-on experience in software development, project management, and emerging technologies.

In addition to technical skills, the MCA program emphasizes the importance of soft skills, such as communication, teamwork, and problem-solving abilities, which are essential for success in the corporate environment. Our faculty comprises experienced professionals and academicians who are dedicated to mentoring students and guiding them throughout their academic journey.

Upon completion of the MCA program, graduates are well-prepared to pursue careers in software development, IT consultancy, system analysis, and many other fields within the technology sector.
